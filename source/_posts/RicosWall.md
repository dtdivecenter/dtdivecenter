### 薄荷島-巴里卡薩-Rico's Wall
<!--more-->
這篇介紹薄荷島-巴里卡薩-Rico's Wall潛點。

### 潛點介紹
```sh
距離     : 從Alona Beach出發約11km 
航行時間 : 出發約30分鐘
地形     : 海牆潛水
證照     : OW以上
能見度   : 15-30公尺
深度     : 5-40公尺
建議     : 10公尺深度最豐富，30公尺以下幾乎無生物。
方向     : 從左邊的海牆向南或右肩向北開始潛水

Description:
Drop-wall in front of the resort on the west side of the island.
Dive site has many small caverns, ledges and reef over-hangings.
If you are going in north direction you probably finish the dive
in the dive site Cathedral and if your dive in south direction
you finish at the dive site Rudy’s rock.

Marine Life:
Large school’s of Vlamming’s Unicorn fish and silver Jack’s Big
eye Trevally that are swimming in circles like a tornado Midnight
Snapper and many different kinds of big size Sweetlip fishes.
```
